You can upload multiple files at a time, either by selecting them in the file browser or by dragging them together onto the upload areas.
  • Column 1 of the uploaded files will be interpreted as time in hours.
  • Column 2 will be interpreted as the observations to place on the y-axis. The column name will be used for the chart label.
  • Column 3, if present, will be used for error bars.
If given, the units for the left and right axis will only be used as labels.

  • Growth data with error bars: FC of Bacteroides thetaiotaomicron VPI-5482 in Average(bhbtri)
  • Baranyi-Roberts model of the above: Baranyi-Roberts predictions
